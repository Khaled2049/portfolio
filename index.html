Who am I?

A Programmer | Life long Learner

I enjoy programming, learning new technologies, and most importantly working with others. There's no doubt programming is fun and challenging but in the end, it is only a tool. I tend to work better when I know what I'm working for and how it will provide value.

My journey started at an early age having two older brothers both computer engineers, I naturally gravitated towards coding, watching my first program run, realizing that I have the ability to make the computer do almost anything I can imagine only fueled my desire to learn more and become a problem solver.

I want to find innovative solutions to the problems we experience. To make the world a better place, work hard and be proud of the things I do.
